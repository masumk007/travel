Place And Information

China

Relics found in the city's Baojiyan (simplified Chinese: 宝积岩; traditional Chinese: 寶積巖) and Zengpiyan (甑皮巖) caves date back to approximately 10,000 years ago. The Zengpiyan people had a matriarchal clan society. Before the Qin dynasty, Guilin region was settled by the Baiyue people. In 314 BC, a small settlement was established along the banks of the Li River.

Switzerland

Prior to the COVID-19 pandemic, obtaining a Schengen visa to visit your partner in Switzerland may have been an extended process, but it was not impossible. But due to the travel restrictions imposed by the pandemic, many unmarried inter-national couples have been separated for months, as EU laws only allowed reunification for married spouses or those in a registered partnership.
